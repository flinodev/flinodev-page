### Description

This web system has all the features of a traditional ecommerce, from viewing the complete product catalog, shopping cart, online payments, product catalog and user administration, authentication, route protection and more. Developed with Next as a full-stack framework, Postgres as database, JWT and Tailwind.

### Features

#### Home

This screen shows a paginated view of available products in the virtual store. In the top bar, at the center you can filter content by categories. Although the system has authentication, this page can be viewed unauthenticated.

![Teslo-shop home](/images/projects/home-teslo.webp)

#### Product Detail

For each product, the most relevant information is shown, such as price, quantity of products in stock, available sizes and a description. Plus the functionality to add it to the shopping cart.

![Product detail in teslo-shop](/images/projects/product-teslo.webp)

#### Shopping Cart

After adding some products, they are stored in the shopping cart for later purchase. Storage is done through the browser's localStorage. Therefore, for its use, it is not necessary to be authenticated.

![Shopping cart in teslo-shop](/images/projects/cart-teslo.webp)

#### Login

To make payment for products, and perform a set of operations beyond exploring the product catalog, authentication is necessary.

![Authentication in teslo-shop](/images/projects/auth-teslo.webp)

#### Checkout

To confirm the order it is necessary to add the delivery address for the products, which we have the option to save in the database (Remember address), or not, in which case it will only be stored in the browser's localStorage.

![Checkout in teslo-shop](/images/projects/checkout-teslo.webp)

After verification of our order, it is stored in the database and is ready to be paid. Confirmation of our order impacts the stock of our products, so that other users when requesting the content of our products can see the availability of products up to that moment.

![Checkout 2 in teslo-shop](/images/projects/checkout-2-teslo.webp)

#### Online Payments

The ecommerce system supports online payments through Paypal as well as debit and credit cards.

![Online payments in teslo-shop](/images/projects/paypal-teslo.webp)

#### Product Catalog Administration

This page, protected and allowed only for administrator users, allows maintenance of the product catalog. Both editing existing ones and adding new products.

![Product administration teslo-shop](/images/projects/admin-products-teslo.webp)

For each product we can modify any information field including photographs. These changes will be persistent in the database and will be reflected for all users who visit the site.

![Product editing in teslo-shop](/images/projects/edit-product-teslo.webp)
